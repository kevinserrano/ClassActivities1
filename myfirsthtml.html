How To Make A PB&J Sandwhich

  1. Get 2 bread slices along with a butter knife, peanutbutter and jelly.
  2. On one side of bread spread the peanutbutter and on the other slice spread the jelly.
  3. Put the slices of bread together with PB&J against eachother.
  4. ENJOY!!!
